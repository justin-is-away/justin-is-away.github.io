Why you should watch Bojack Horseman, maybe



Bojack Horseman is a fantastic show and one of my personal favorites. It follows the life of a washed up actor who happens to be a very belligerent horse.


The show masterfully explores realistic and problematic dynamics found in everyday life and society, despite it being set in a wacky universe with anthropomorphized creatures.


The characters and their personal development is amazing, the show's callbacks and attention to detail are immaculate, and the jokes are pretty funny.


Also, there are clever puns and Easter eggs.


So why do I say you should maybe watch it?


  • The show can be kind of depressing.
  • The humor can be hit and miss; it references a lot of Hollywood or movie content.
  • The animation style isn't for everyone.
  • There isn't an obviously linear plot, but you can't watch episodes out of order because everything ties back together.
